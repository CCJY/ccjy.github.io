## Vector Intro

### vector - magnitude and direction

---

>$$ 
\begin{aligned}
\left. \begin{aligned}
\mathbf {R}^2
\end{aligned}\right\rbrace 
\texttt{2-dimensional real coordinate space}
\end{aligned}
$$
### $\mathbf{R}^2$- 2-dismensional real coordinate -> all possible real-valued 2-tuple
>$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{v} &= (5,0) = 
\begin{bmatrix}
5 \\ 0
\end{bmatrix} 
\end{aligned}
$$
### $ \mathbf{R}^3 $- 3D real coordinate space -> real-valued 3-tuples 
>$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{x} =
\begin{bmatrix}
-1 \\
5 \\
3
\end{bmatrix}
\\
\\
\vec{x} \in \mathbf{R}^3 \\
\end{aligned}
$$
### Not real valued tuple because of i
>$$ 
\begin{aligned}
\vec{c} =
\begin{bmatrix}
i \\
0\\
1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$
